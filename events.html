Fundraising

Do you have a deep affinity for music? Love supporting your local community? What better way to do that than participating in our local fundraising events!

We host bi-monthly concerts, both in-person and online via YouTube, to fundraise for our music students.

Do you have unused instruments sitting in your closets? Sheet music? Instrument cases or old church clothes and shoes?

We host monthly instrument drives! Please follow the directory to see when and where our next drive is located. We'd love to see you there!

Strings of Solace

A non-profit orchestra that travels across the country, playing music in low-income communities.
Comprised of mostly veterans and retired law enforcement, Strings of Solace gives it's members a safe place to experience the healing powers of music, as well as share that with their community.

Tickets are $35, $45 at the door.
