Enabling our youth through the power of music and community ♬

Musical Means Foundation has a strong passion for incorporating music in schools and communities who cannot afford the joys of learning and performing music.

Our goal: to raise resources and fund the local community of aspiring musicians, who cannot otherwise afford lessons, instruments, transportation or performance attire. They also provide resources, such as sheet music and online lessons.

If you would like to donate, monetarily or instrument-wise, please visit our Donate page!

Every week, a young aspiring musician is gifted with free lessons, a sponsor, their own instrument or even means of transportation to events or said lessons. Without the constant support from our community and members, these children would never experience the joy, discipline and escape that music brings.

Our students are blessed everyday by donators just like you!
